Learn The History

Tank Man, also known as the Unknown Protester or Unknown Rebel, is the nickname of an unidentified Chinese man who stood in front of a column of tanks leaving Tiananmen Square on June 5, 1989, the day after the Chinese military had suppressed the Tiananmen Square protests.
